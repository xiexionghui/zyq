企业简介

COMPANY PROFILE

　　深圳市联合蓝海黄金材料科技股份有限公司是深圳市联合蓝海投资控股集团的下属企业，成立于2010年5月25日，是一家专注于无氰环保镀金工艺技术的研发、服务、推广应用及智能制造技术研发的国家级高新技术企业。

　　Shenzhen United Blueocean Gold Material Technology Co, Ltd (UBO) was established on May 25, 2010 as a subsidiary company of Shenzhen United Blueocean Investment Holding Group. Now, UBO has grown up into a national high-tech enterprise specializing in R&D, service, promotion and application of eco-friendly and cyanide-free gold plating technology and intelligent manufacturing technology.

　　公司拥有完全自主知识产权的无氰环保镀金工艺技术体系，并已获得8项国家发明专利（含1项国防专利）和1项国际发明专利，被世界黄金协会评价为“黄金材料学上的一次革命”，其中 2项技术被国家工信部、科技部授予《科学技术成果登记证书》。该工艺技术体系荣获了2017年广东省环境保护科学技术二等奖，2018年深圳市科技进步二等奖，可广泛应用于航空航天、电子元件、军工产品、珠宝、钟表、礼品、奢侈品和五金卫浴等领域。

　　The Cyanide-free gold plating process technology system is developed by UBO itself and hold with completely independent intellectual property rights by UBO and has obtained 8 national invention patents (including 1 national defense patent) and 1 international invention patent. The technology system was evaluated as "a revolution in the science of gold material” by the World Gold Council. 2 items of this system have been awarded "Certificate of Registration of Scientific and Technological Achievements" by the National Ministry of Industry and Information and the Ministry of Science and Technology. The Cyanide-free gold plating technology system won the second prize of Guangdong Environmental Protection Science and Technology in 2017 and the second prize of Shenzhen Science and Technology Progress Award in 2018. It can be widely used in aerospace, electronic components, military products, jewelry, watches, gifts, luxury goods, hardware, sanitary products and many other industry and fields.

　　公司已将该工艺技术成功地应用于装饰性镀金领域最广泛的3D硬金饰品行业，并实现了工业规模化生产，形成了年产6吨无氰硬金产品的生产能力。生产过程节能、环保、无污染，产品不含有毒有害元素，产品已通过国家环境标志产品认证证书。现已向珠宝行业周大福、国金黄金、东莞信泰企业投资管理集团有限公司等知名品牌公司转让了全套工艺技术，并已实现工业化量产。

　　UBO has successfully applied this process technology system to the 3D hard gold jewelry industry, the most widely used in the field of decorative gold plating. Meanwhile, it has achieved industrial mass production forming an annual production capacity of 6 tons of cyanide-free hard gold products. The production process is energy saving, eco-friendly, which ensures that the product does not contain toxic and harmful elements, and thus has passed the national environmental labeling product certification. Now UBO has transferred the complete process technology system to the well-known brand jewelry companies such as Chow Tai Fook, Guo Jin Gold, Dongguan Xintai and they have already achieved industrial mass production.

　　公司正致力于智能制造系统的研发，现已实现了C端（消费端）到M端（制造端）的毫秒级信息传输和批量化私人定制。

　　UBO is currently working on the development of intelligent manufacturing system and has realized the millisecond-level information transmission and mass customization of the C-end (consumer end) to the M-end (manufacturing side).

　　通过多年努力，我司已通过ISO9001质量管理体系、ISO14001环境管理体系、知识产权管理体系、能源管理体系认证，并连续3年（2013-2015年）蝉联JNA金奖、2016年荣获了第19届世界表面精饰大会“创新型企业”和国家级高新技术企业称号。

　　Through years of great efforts, UBO has passed ISO9001 quality management system, ISO14001 environmental management system, intellectual property management system, certification of energy management system, and won the JNA Gold Award for three consecutive years (2013-2015). It has also been honored as the “Innovative Enterprise” of the 19th World Surface Finishing Conference in 2016 and the title of national high-tech enterprise.
